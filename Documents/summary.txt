SUMMARY OF WORLD NEWS: JUNE 16, 2026
====================================

1. International Diplomacy & Conflict:
   - A preliminary US–Iran peace agreement was announced by US President Donald Trump at the G7 summit. The deal features a 60-day ceasefire and the reopening of the Strait of Hormuz, causing oil prices to fall. A formal signing is set for June 19 in Geneva.
   - Israeli PM Netanyahu expressed skepticism and insisted Israel will protect its own security.
   - The EU adopted a new sanctions package targeting Russia's "shadow fleet" and energy sector.

2. Accidents & Disasters:
   - A US B-52 military aircraft crashed during a test flight in California; all eight crew members are feared dead.
   - Two significant earthquakes struck Qinghai, China (M6.3), and Palu, Indonesia (M6.7), resulting in two total casualties and multiple injuries.
   - A train-bus collision in Zimbabwe resulted in nine deaths.

3. Domestic & Economic Policies:
   - UK Prime Minister Starmer announced plans to ban social media platforms for children under 16.
   - The Bank of Japan raised interest rates to 1%, its highest rate since 1995.
   - The US DOJ charged 15 people following protests during a Minneapolis immigration operation.
